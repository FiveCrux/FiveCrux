Overview

A complete economy backbone for your server

Advanced Banking System brings a polished, performance-first financial layer to your FiveM server. Players manage their money through interactive ATMs scattered across the map, take out and repay loans with configurable interest, and run shared society accounts for jobs and gangs — all synced server-side with full transaction logging.

Built natively for both ESX and QBCore, the script ships with a clean NUI that matches modern server aesthetics and stays under 0.02ms idle. Every label, interest rate, and permission is exposed in a single readable config.
