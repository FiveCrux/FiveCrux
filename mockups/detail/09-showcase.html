Overview

A complete, performance-first banking economy for your server. Advanced Banking System brings fully-featured ATMs across the map, a clean in-game bank UI, peer-to-peer transfers, and a robust loan engine with configurable interest and repayment schedules. Built for roleplay depth without the bloat.

Run a real economy with society accounts for jobs and gangs, shared balances, payroll, and full transaction logging. Every interaction is optimised to stay at 0.00ms idle, and the entire system is database-driven with clean exports for your other resources.

Features

Map-wide ATMs

Withdraw, deposit & check balance at every ATM and bank branch.

Loan engine

Configurable interest, limits and automated repayment schedules.

Society accounts

Shared balances, payroll & permissions for jobs and gangs.

Full transaction logs

Every transfer logged with Discord webhook support.

Mobile transfers

Player-to-player payments straight from the phone.

0.00ms idle

Database-driven and fully optimised for busy servers.

  • oxmysql 2.0+
  • FiveM server build 5848+
