Overview

A complete, production-ready banking framework for your FiveM server. Players manage personal and business finances through a polished interface — deposit and withdraw at physical ATMs scattered across the map, take out and repay loans with configurable interest, and run shared society accounts for jobs, gangs and organizations.

Designed for performance and clarity: every transaction is logged, every UI screen is responsive, and the entire system drops into both ESX and QBCore with zero conflicts. Configure interest rates, ATM placements and society permissions from a single readable config.

Features

Physical ATMs & banks
Configurable props, blips and interaction zones across the map.
Loans & interest
Credit limits, repayment schedules and automatic debit.
Society accounts
Shared balances for jobs, gangs and businesses.
Player transfers
Instant transfers with full transaction history.
