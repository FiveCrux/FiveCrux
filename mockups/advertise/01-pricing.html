FAQ

Common questions

What's the difference between the two ad products?

Banner Ad Slots are rotating image placements across the marketplace and listing pages. Featured Script Slots boost one of your scripts into the Featured rows and homepage spotlight.

Can I change my creative after going live?

Yes. Swap banners or change the boosted script anytime from your dashboard — changes go through a quick review and update within hours.

How is CTR measured?

CTR is clicks divided by impressions, updated in real time. The marketplace currently averages 8.4% across active ad slots.

Do prices include both products?

Every tier includes banner slots, and Premium and Executive add Featured Script Slots. You can also buy products individually from your dashboard.
